My Story & Journey

The person behind the code

Hello! I am a software engineer based in the beautiful city of Kathmandu. My journey in technology started when I realized the power code has to not just solve logical problems, but to fundamentally improve human experiences.

As a Full Stack Developer and UI/UX enthusiast, I bridge the gap between aesthetic design and robust engineering. For over 5 years, I've worked primarily with JavaScript ecosystems, Python architectures, and cloud deployments, focusing on building user-centric, accessible, and highly-scalable solutions.

Beyond staring at a code editor, my passions are quite diverse. I love to hike through the Himalayas, experiment with specialty coffee brewing, and mentor junior developers in local tech communities. Teaching others is how I continuously learn and refine my own skills.
